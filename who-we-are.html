Our Story

Who We Are

A covenant community rooted in the way of Jesus, for the sake of Chapel Hill and the world.

Our Mission

Becoming a people

CityWell exists to become a people who receive the ways of Jesus as our way of life, for the sake of His glory, and for the sake of the world. That single sentence — our mission — shapes everything we do.

We're not a religious institution trying to recruit members. We're a family of flawed, hopeful people learning to be formed by Jesus together. We fail at this regularly. We try again. We forgive and move forward.

We're located in Chapel Hill, NC — home to UNC, Duke, a thriving arts scene, and a deeply diverse community of students, faculty, artists, and families. We love this place and we're trying to love it well.

"For all who thirst — the words over our door are not a marketing slogan. They are a promise."
— CityWell Community
