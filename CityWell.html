First Visit

New to
CityWell?

We're glad you found us. CityWell is a warm, welcoming community in Chapel Hill — we'd love for you to join us on a Sunday. No expectations, just an open door.

Plan your visit
01

Come as you are

No dress code, no expectations. Show up exactly as you are — our Sunday service is casual, warm, and about 75 minutes long.

02

Children are welcome

CityBabies (0–3) and CityKids (3–10) run during the service. Kids are also always welcome to stay with you in worship.

03

Belong before you believe

At CityWell, you don't have to have it all figured out. We believe belonging comes before believing — join us at the table.
