Who We Are Together

Contributing your gifts

The church is a body — and a body only works when every part does its part. Whether your gifts are musical, relational, organizational, or practical, there is a place for you to serve at CityWell.

Worship

Music & Arts

Our worship team leads the congregation each Sunday. Vocalists, instrumentalists, and sound/tech volunteers are always welcome.
