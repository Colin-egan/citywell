Why We Give

Life is most beautiful when shared

Generosity is one of the central practices of the covenant way. When we give, we are enacting what we believe: that we don't live for ourselves alone, that everything we have is held on loan, and that there is always enough to share.

Your giving to CityWell funds Sunday worship, our Wells groups, children's ministry, pastoral care, community outreach, and our ongoing work in Chapel Hill. Every dollar goes toward building a community of belonging and life in this city.

We never pressure anyone to give — especially guests. If you're visiting, please don't feel any expectation. If you feel called to contribute, we receive every gift with deep gratitude.
