Join Us

We gather every Sunday and throughout the week — here's how to find your place.

Every Sunday

Worship Together

We gather each Sunday at 10:30am for an hour of worship, teaching from Scripture, and community. Our services are informal, joyful, and open to everyone — wherever you are on your spiritual journey.

Children's ministry is available during the service for ages 0–10. We also livestream for those who can't make it in person.

When & Where

Service Time

Sundays at 10:30am

Our Sunday worship gathering typically runs about 75 minutes. Arrive a few minutes early and we'll have coffee waiting.

Location

Chapel Hill, NC

We meet in the heart of Chapel Hill. See our contact page for the full address, parking info, and a map.

Children

CityBabies & CityKids

Safe, welcoming environments for little ones ages 0–10. Kids are always welcome to stay with their families in the service too.
