Children's Ministry · Ages 3–10

CityKids

A place where children discover they are known and loved by God.

For Growing Kids

Faith that sticks

CityKids is our Sunday morning program for children ages 3–10. Kids are dismissed after our opening worship to their own space, where they participate in age-appropriate teaching, creative activities, games, and prayer.

We don't just teach kids facts about God — we help them experience belonging to a community that takes the way of Jesus seriously. We want faith to be something they own, not just something their parents brought them to.

The CityKids Experience

Teaching

Scripture & Story

We use an age-appropriate curriculum that walks children through the story of Scripture in a way that's engaging, honest, and full of wonder.

Community

Real Friendships

CityKids is also about kids building relationships with each other. The friendships formed here often last well into adolescence and beyond.

Safety

Safe & Secure

All volunteers are background-checked and trained. We follow safe-church protocols and use a secure check-in and check-out system every Sunday.

Want to volunteer with CityKids?

Our children's ministry runs because of volunteers who love kids. We'd love to hear from you.
