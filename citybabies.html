Children's Ministry · Ages 0–3

CityBabies

A warm, safe space for your littlest ones while you worship.

For Your Youngest

A place for little ones

CityBabies is our nursery and toddler program for children ages birth through 3 years. We know that when parents feel confident their child is safe and cared for, they can truly participate in worship — and that's what we want for your whole family.

Our volunteers are background-checked, trained in safe-church practices, and genuinely love being with little ones. We use a simple pager/notification system so you can step out if needed.

What to Know

Check-In

Simple & Secure

Check in at the CityBabies desk before the service. You'll receive a secure tag that matches your child's tag — required for pickup.

Environment

Safe & Nurturing

Our rooms are cleaned before each service. Volunteers are trained, background-checked, and always paired (never alone with children).

What to Bring

Pack What You Need

Bring your child's diaper bag with labeled bottles, diapers, and any comfort items. We'll make sure they're happy and well-cared-for.

Questions about CityBabies?

We'd love to connect you with our children's ministry team before your first Sunday.
